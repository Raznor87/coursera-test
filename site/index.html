Most elements have opening and closing tags:

<some_element_name>some_content</some_element_name>

But some only have a singular tag:

e.g.: 'some_content<br>' causes a linebreak after 'some_content'

Tag attributes are declared within the opening tag:

<some_element_name an_attribute_name="an_attribute_value">some_content</some_element_name>

Spaces are not allowed after the opening brackets of tags, and there must be a space between the element name and the attribute name.
Other spaces are simply ignored.
Quote rules inside attribute values are almost like in Python.

This is the footer after the horizontal rule, indicated by the tag '<hr>'.
